Our Story

Niyantri Cafe began as a small bean-to-crew cafe in Hyderabad, inspired by the comforting aroma of chocolate and the joy of slow moments with friends. We design each corner to feel like a warm hug—plush seats, cocoa-toned walls, and pastries that melt in your mouth.

Our name, Niyantri, evokes a mindful, welcoming space where every guest can pause, savor, and feel at home. We source ethically, roast thoughtfully, and bake with love to create a cafe that tastes like a memory you return to.
